Welcome to WildlifeWeb

This platform allows people from all over the nation to engage in local wildlife by uploading and discovering sightings.

By uploading your sightings, you're contributing to science! The more data we have, the better understanding of our natural environment.

WildlifeWeb aims to support citizen science efforts, especially in urban areas, and increase awareness of wildlife among the public. Together, we can make a significant impact on understanding and preserving our local ecosystems.

Discover

On the Discover tab, you can explore a massive collection of sightings provided by like-minded nature enthusiasts. Learn about different wildlife spotted in your area and expand your knowledge about various species. Our database is continuously updated with the latest sightings, making it a great resource for wildlife enthusiasts and researchers alike.
