How to Use KML Files on Google Maps

Follow these steps to import and use KML files with Google My Maps and Google Maps.


Step 1: Download the KML File

Click on the provided KML file link to download the file onto your device. The file should be saved in your "Downloads" folder or a location you choose.

Step 2: Open Google My Maps

Go to Google My Maps on your web browser. Sign in with your Google account if you haven't already.

Step 3: Create a New Map

Click on the "Create a new map" button.

Step 4: Import the KML File

In the new map, click on the "Import" button. Then, select the KML file you downloaded in Step 1.

Step 5: Save the Map

Once the KML file is imported, you can see the route on the map. Give your map a name by clicking on "Untitled map" and entering a new name. Click "Save" to save your map.

Step 6: Open the Map in Google Maps

Open the Google Maps app on your mobile device. Tap on the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top-left corner, then go to "Your places" -> "Maps". You should see the map you created with the imported KML file. Tap on it to view the route.

Step 7: Use the Map for Navigation

You can now use the imported map for navigation. Tap on the route or waypoints to get directions and start your walk or drive along the specified path.
